RIP to the legend, Kobe Bryant. He was one of my favorite players to ever play the game. He was my first favorite player actually. He always gave his all on the court til he had nothin left. He will live on in basketball forever



Some of Kobe's greatest accomplishments include:



  • 5x NBA Champion
  • Most Points scored in a single game in the modern era with 81
  • 19x NBA All Star
  • 2x FMVP
  • 25-5-5 career averages
  • Once Outscored the entire Dallas team 62-61 through just 3 quarters of play
  • 2008 NBA MVP
  • 2x Scoring champ
  • Lakers All Time Leader in Points Scored
  • 15x All NBA team Member
  • 13x All defensive team member
